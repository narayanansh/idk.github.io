Who We Are ?

Since its creation in 2002, LAB CRYSTALS has provided high-quality geological sample preparation and analytical services for clients from industrial and academic sectors worldwide. Lab Crystals offers a comprehensive range of services. Petrography Slide Preparation and Interpretation Petrographic services at the LAB CRYSTALS include full-service petrographic sample preparation (polished and standard thin sections), using a wide range of modern polishing and grinding equipment. Samples are prepared to high standards from various types of rock and minerals, which can provide valuable insight useful in determining exploration and economic potential.

Key Features

• Wide range of manual lapping and polishing equipment in order to successfully process difficult samples
• Precision lapping and polishing equipment ensures samples are prepared to optimal thickness and flatness Lab Crystals offers a materials preparation service producing petrographic thin sections from samples and specimens of
• Rock
• Aggregate
• Fossil / coral
• Concrete and other similar materials
